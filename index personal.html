What do i want from coding?

However, it wasnt as easy as i had initially thought, i spent weeks trying and tweaking opertunities and methods to get excel do do what i was asking, constantly scouring the web on why i was getting these errors and this constant bombardment of blocks and failed access. Denied! overwhelmed with the emotions of the highs and lows i had experienced on this idea for weeks at this point, i once more reached out to my central team in hopes of aid, and this time.. was still told no but directed to a diffrent platform of individuals within the compay who are all in the tech department on slack. now this, was, eye, opening!
